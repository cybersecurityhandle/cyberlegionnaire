Endpoint & Network Security

Full-spectrum detection and response across every endpoint, network segment, and cloud workload in your environment. Our team monitors around the clock so threats are contained before they cause damage.

  • EDR / XDR deployment and tuning
  • Network traffic analysis and anomaly detection
  • Cloud security posture management
  • 24/7 alert monitoring and triage
  • Incident containment and remediation

Digital Forensics & Incident Response

When a breach happens, speed and accuracy matter. We deploy immediately to contain, investigate, and recover — then deliver a full root cause analysis so it doesn't happen again.

  • Rapid containment and isolation
  • Forensic evidence collection and preservation
  • Malware analysis and reverse engineering
  • Post-incident root cause report
  • Recovery planning and hardening recommendations

Vulnerability Management

Continuous scanning and prioritization across your entire infrastructure. We identify what's exploitable, rank it by real-world risk, and guide remediation before adversaries act.

  • Authenticated and unauthenticated scanning
  • Risk-based prioritization (CVSS + context)
  • Patch guidance and remediation tracking
  • Recurring scan cadence and trend reporting

Tabletop Exercises

Realistic breach simulations run with your leadership and technical teams. We stress-test your incident response plans, expose gaps, and leave you with a clear action list.

  • Custom scenarios based on your threat profile

  • Gap analysis and improvement roadmap

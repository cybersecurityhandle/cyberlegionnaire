Authorized adversary simulation to find and fix your vulnerabilities before real attackers exploit them.

Penetration Testing

Controlled, authorized attacks on your systems to identify exploitable vulnerabilities. We deliver clear, actionable reports — not just a CVE dump.

  • Web application and API testing
  • Internal and external network testing
  • Wireless and physical security assessments
  • Social engineering and phishing simulations
  • Executive summary + technical remediation report

Red Teaming

Full-scope, objective-based adversary simulations. We operate like a real threat actor — targeting your people, processes, and technology over weeks to find what a penetration test misses.

  • Custom threat actor emulation
  • Physical, digital, and social attack vectors
  • Assume-breach and full kill-chain scenarios
  • Stealth operations to test detection capabilities
  • Detailed findings with attack narrative

Purple Teaming

Red and blue working together in real time. Attackers probe, defenders respond, and both teams close gaps immediately — building detection capabilities that last beyond the engagement.

  • Collaborative adversary simulation sessions
  • MITRE ATT&CK framework mapping
  • Detection rule development and tuning
  • Measurable improvement against defined TTPs

Threat Actor Profiling

Bespoke intelligence on the specific threat actors most likely to target your organization — their TTPs, infrastructure, motivations, and known attack patterns.

  • Sector-specific threat landscape analysis
  • Named actor TTP mapping
  • Indicators of compromise (IOC) packages
  • Defensive prioritization recommendations
